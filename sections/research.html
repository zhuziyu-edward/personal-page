Research Interests

3D Vision-Language Learning Embodied AI Spatial Understanding World Model Multimodal Learning

My research centers on building intelligent systems that can perceive, understand, and interact with the 3D physical world through natural language. I explore how multimodal learning can bridge vision and language in 3D environments, enabling embodied agents to perform complex spatial reasoning and manipulation tasks. I am also interested in developing world models that allow AI systems to predict and plan in dynamic 3D scenes, paving the way for more capable and generalizable embodied intelligence.
